Collection Barq

Everything your leaders need, in one place

Collection Barq is the unified internal hub for Barq's leaders — bringing the contact directory, escalation channels, quick links, and ready-to-send HR forms together in one fast, simple workspace. Reaching the right person at the right time has never been easier.

👥

Leaders Directory

Find any leader instantly — name, role, department, email, phone, and a one-tap Microsoft Teams chat.

🔗

Escalation Links

Direct, one-click access to every key escalation channel and its tracker — no digging through folders.

⚡

Quick Access

Sprinklr, ITSM, Microsoft Teams, Outlook, and the company intranet — all reachable from a single screen.

📄

Smart HR Forms

Generate violation, resignation, and leave requests in seconds — pre-filled, signed, and ready to send.

🌐

Fully Bilingual

Work in Arabic or English with instant right-to-left switching across the entire experience.

🔒

Secure by Design

Protected by company sign-in — only approved @barq.com accounts can get inside.

Simple from the first click. No training and no manuals — open the hub, find what you need, and get back to work. Built to feel fast on any device, for every leader on the team.
